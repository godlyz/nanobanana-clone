## Content Guidelines

### Allowed Content

- AI-generated art including images, videos, animations

- Creative experiences, tool guides, tips sharing

- AI art discussions, inspiration sharing

- Personal AI art portfolios

### Prohibited Content

- Content violating local laws and regulations
- Content infringing intellectual property (unauthorized piracy, plagiarism)

- Pornographic, nude, sexually suggestive content
- Inappropriate content targeting minors

- Extremely violent, gory, abusive content
- Self-harm or content harming others

- Deliberately spreading false, misleading information
- Maliciously manipulating AI-generated content to mislead others

- Content inciting crime, violence, or dangerous behavior
- Content promoting dangerous items or activities

## Creation Guidelines

### AI Tool Usage Guidelines

   - Recommended to credit AI tools used (e.g., Google Veo, DALL-E)
   - For hybrid creations (AI + manual editing), explain the creative process

   - Encourage creating unique prompts and ideas
   - Avoid simply copying others' prompts or works

   - Upload clear, complete works
   - Avoid uploading low-quality, unfinished drafts (unless marked as "experimental")

### Copyright and Attribution

   - Do not upload unauthorized copyrighted content
   - When using reference images, ensure you have usage rights or comply with fair use

   - If using others' prompts, styles, or inspiration, acknowledge in description
   - When using famous artist styles, note "Style reference: [Artist Name]"

   - If works are for commercial purposes, state in privacy settings or description
   - Comply with Nano Banana's Terms of Service regarding commercial use

## Social Interaction Guidelines

### Comment Guidelines

- Provide specific, helpful feedback
- Point out highlights and areas for improvement

- Use friendly, respectful tone
- Be polite even if you don't like the work

- Malicious criticism, personal attacks
- Spam comments (meaningless content like "First!")

### Following and Engagement

- Follow creators you're genuinely interested in
- Like and comment on works you're interested in

- Follow-for-follow deals
- Using automated tools for mass following/liking

### Direct Message Guidelines

- Send friendly, professional messages
- Respect others' response time

- Harassing, threatening messages
- Unauthorized commercial promotions

## Reporting and Moderation

### How to Report Violations


- Click "Report" button on artwork detail page
- Select violation type (spam, inappropriate content, copyright infringement, etc.)
- Provide detailed explanation (optional)

- Click "Report" icon next to comment
- Select violation reason

- Visit user profile, click "Report User"
- Describe violation behavior and evidence

- For serious violations (illegal content, violent threats), email immediately to:
  - **support@nanobanana.com**

### Moderation Process

   - System automatically detects and hides obvious violations

   - Moderation team reviews all reports within 24 hours
   - Complex cases may take longer

   - Reporters will receive notification of action taken
   - Violators will receive warning or penalty notice

### Appeal Process



If you believe your content was mistakenly removed or account wrongly banned:

1. Visit `/appeal` page to submit appeal
2. Provide detailed reasons and evidence
3. Appeal team will review and respond within 7 business days

## Violation Handling

### Warning System


- Warning + content removal
- Account functions retained

- Warning + 7-day feature restriction (no posting, commenting)
- Violation record kept for 30 days

- Permanent account ban
- All content removed

### Immediate Ban for Serious Violations


- Child pornography, human trafficking, or other serious illegal content
- Real threats or incitement to violence
- Large-scale spam attacks
- Malicious use of automated tools to disrupt community
